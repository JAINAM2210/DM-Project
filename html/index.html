What we do

Music to DNA Conversion

Music Analysis

We utilize advanced algorithms and techniques to analyze music compositions and extract key musical elements.

DNA Encoding

We convert the analyzed music data into a unique DNA sequence, representing the musical composition. We also employ the RSA algorithm to encrypt and decrypt data, ensuring secure transmission and storage.

DNA Synthesis

Using state-of-the-art laboratory techniques, we synthesize the DNA sequence derived from the music analysis.

DNA Storage

The synthesized DNA is securely stored, preserving the musical composition in a long-term and compact form.
